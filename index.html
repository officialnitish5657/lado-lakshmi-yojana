Lado Lakshmi Yojana – Haryana Govt. Scheme

Empowering women with financial independence

Overview

The Lado Lakshmi Yojana is a Haryana government initiative aimed at empowering women by providing ₹2100 per month to eligible women from economically weaker sections.

Objectives of the Scheme

  • Empower Women: Financial support to help women become self-reliant.
  • Promote Education & Health: Raise awareness about education and health.
  • Support Small Businesses: Enable women to start small businesses.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Gender: Only women can apply.
  • Age: Minimum 18 years.
  • Economic Status: Women from BPL families with income under ₹1.8 lakh/year.
  • Residency: Permanent residents of Haryana.
  • Employment Status: Not applicable to women in government jobs or already receiving similar aid.

Financial Assistance

Eligible women will receive ₹2100 per month, directly deposited into their bank accounts to improve economic conditions and promote self-sufficiency.

Application Process

  • Online: Apply through the official Haryana government portal once enrollment begins.
  • Offline: Visit the nearest Women & Child Development Department office to apply.

Required Documents

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Permanent Residence Certificate
  • BPL Card (if applicable)
  • Bank Account Details
  • Passport-size Photo
  • Income Certificate
